I don’t think there are any disadvantages to acquiring a language using methodologies that aim to approach the way native speakers acquire their own languages. Language analysis and acquisition, as you know, are not the same. The way I see it, ‘living language’ methods should be utilized in programs to give students direct access to and experience in the language for the first few semesters, and then, after a degree of fluency (ideally a high degree), they will be better equipped to engage in full and deeper analysis.
